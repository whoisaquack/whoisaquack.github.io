About Me

I am Kevin Bass and I love ducks. Quack. My pastime consists of being open-minded about things I agree with and selflessly helping people understand more about health and nutrition. Some call me a philantrope; others call me their personal hero. If you ask me, I'm just another guy trying to do their part of good in this world.

When it comes to science I am all about cold hard data, and I especially like it when the data comes in spreadsheets so that even normal people can understand it. Science is all about sharing knowledge, you see.
